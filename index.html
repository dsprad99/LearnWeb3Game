What is Web3?

Web3 applications either run on blockchains, decentralized networks of many peer to peer nodes (servers), or a combination of the two that forms a cryptoeconomic protocol. These apps are often referred to as dapps (decentralized apps), and you will see that term used often in the web3 space.

What is cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual currency that uses cryptography (encryption techniques) to secure and verify transactions and to control the creation of new units. It operates independently of a central bank or government and uses a decentralized system of record-keeping called a blockchain to maintain a public ledger of all transactions.
